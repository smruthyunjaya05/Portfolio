Pedestrian AI

Real-time Pedestrian Detection for Android

Pedestrian AI is an advanced Android application that utilizes YOLOv8 and TensorFlow Lite for real-time pedestrian detection. This system helps enhance road safety by providing instant alerts and precise detection of pedestrians in various conditions.

Features

  • Real-time pedestrian detection
  • High accuracy with YOLOv8
  • Optimized for mobile devices
  • Night vision capability
  • Distance estimation
  • Audio and visual alerts

Why Pedestrian AI?

Road safety remains a critical concern, particularly in urban areas with high pedestrian traffic. Pedestrian AI addresses this challenge by providing drivers with real-time detection and alerts, potentially preventing accidents and saving lives through early warning systems.

Tech Stack

  • ML Model: YOLOv8
  • Mobile Framework: TensorFlow Lite
  • Computer Vision: OpenCV
  • Platform: Android (Java/Kotlin)
