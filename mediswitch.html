MediSwitch

Smart Alternative Medicine Recommendation System

MediSwitch is an innovative platform that helps users find alternative medicine options based on their current prescriptions. Using machine learning and a comprehensive medicine database, it suggests equivalent alternatives while considering effectiveness, cost, and availability.

Features

  • Smart medicine recommendation system
  • Cost comparison analysis
  • Chatbot assistance with Botpress
  • Comprehensive medicine database
  • Drug interaction warnings
  • Availability tracking

Why MediSwitch?

Rising healthcare costs and medicine shortages often leave patients struggling to find affordable alternatives to their prescribed medications. MediSwitch empowers users with knowledge about equivalent options, helping them make informed decisions about their healthcare while potentially reducing costs.

Tech Stack

  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • ML: Scikit-learn
  • Chatbot: Botpress
  • Database: PostgreSQL
